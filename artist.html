Artist Spotlight: Radiohead

Deep dive into one of the most influential alternative rock bands.

About the Artist

Radiohead is an English rock band formed in Oxfordshire in 1985. They are known for their experimental approach to music, blending alternative rock with electronic and ambient influences.

Featured Album

OK Computer (1997)

A groundbreaking album often considered one of the greatest of all time. It explores themes of technology, isolation, and modern society.

Popular Songs

Paranoid Android

Karma Police

No Surprises

Exit Music (For a Film)

Fun Facts

  • The band originally formed at a school in the 1980s.
  • They are known for constantly reinventing their sound.
  • OK Computer is widely considered one of the greatest albums ever made.
